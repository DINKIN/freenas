Widget Sizes
============

To support automated nice ordering on dashboard. Widgets should be in one of the ten following sizes.

* 75px × 75px
* 165px × 75px
* 165px × 75px
* 255px × 165px
* 345px × 165px
* 255px × 255px
* 345px × 255px
* 435px × 255px
* 345px × 345px
* 705px × 525px
